## 問題 5.4 解答

### a

#### 制御器の命令列

```scm
(controller
    (assign continue (label expt-done))
  expt-loop
    (test (op =) (reg n) (const 0))
    (branch (label base-case))
    (save continue)
    (assign n (op -) (reg n) (const 1))
    (assign continue (label after-expt))
    (goto (label expt-loop))
  after-expt
    (retore continue)
    (assign val (op *) (reg b) (reg val))
    (goto (reg continue))
  base-case
    (assign val (const 1))
    (goto (reg continue))
  expt-done)
```
